1. Brand Identity Overview
1.1 Brand Essence

ShrubHub blends nature, community, and modern simplicity. The identity must always feel:

Fresh and growth-oriented

Friendly and approachable

Clean, modern, minimalistic

Trustworthy and nature-conscious

The logo combines water, waves, energy, and a sprouting plant—symbolizing nourishment, cycles, and growth. This dual symbolism is the heart of the brand.

2. Logo System

2.1 Logo Description

The logo consists of:

Wave & water motif (outer circular form) — symbolizes care, nourishment, and environmental grounding.

Sprouting green plant — represents growth, learning, and cultivation.

Symmetrical root/base geometry — conveys balance, stability, and structure.

It is a standalone symbol; the text lockup (“ShrubHub”) may be applied beneath it or used separately depending on context.

2.2 Logo Construction

Primarily circular form for ease of placement

Line weights taper smoothly, conveying motion and organic shape

Plant leaves use organic gradients that communicate depth and vitality

2.3 Clear Space

Maintain padding equal to half the height of the plant sprout around all sides.
No text, icons, or graphical elements should intrude on this safe area.

2.4 Minimum Size

Digital: 48px height

Print: 12mm height
Below this threshold, leaf definition becomes unclear.

2.5 Approved Lockups

The brand supports two primary configurations:

Symbol only (recommended for app icon, favicon, or badges)

Symbol + Wordmark (“ShrubHub” in Deep Forest Green beneath the symbol)

I can generate polished wordmark variations if you want a standardized lockup.

4. Typography System

Typography should feel open, clean, and contemporary.

4.1 Primary Typeface — Roboto
Style	Size (example)	Weight	Color
Header 1	32–48px	Bold	Deep Forest Green (#228B1B)
Header 2	20–28px	Semibold	Ocean Blue – Deep (#0A6F9C)
Body	14–18px	Regular	Coal Grey (#333333)
Caption	12–14px	Regular	Coal Grey, 70% opacity

Roboto is chosen for its approachability, excellent legibility, and wide style range.

4.2 Typesetting Rules

Line height: 1.35–1.5

Sentence case for headers

Avoid excessive character spacing

Never use condensed fonts (breaks the open, friendly aesthetic)

5. Iconography

Icons seen in the earlier sample (Add Plant, Share, Community, Tasks) should follow these rules:

5.1 Style Guide

Rounded line caps

2–2.5px stroke weight (responsive scaling)

Simple geometry

Avoid 3D, shadows, or overly complex shapes

Colors: primarily Ocean Blue – Deep or Deep Forest Green

5.2 Behavior

Active state: lighten stroke by 10–15%

Disabled state: reduce opacity to 35%

6. UI Components
6.1 Buttons
Primary Button

Background: Deep Forest Green (#228B1B)

Text: White

Radius: 8–10px

Hover: Lighten background 8–12%

Secondary Button

Background: Ocean Blue – Deep (#0A6F9C)

Text: White

Hover: lighten or add outline variant

Tertiary

Text-only

Color: Ocean Blue – Mid (#2DA1C4)

7. Layout & Spacing
Core spacing unit: 8px

Used for margins, padding, and grid spacing.

Container padding

Mobile: 16–24px

Desktop: 24–40px

Keep layouts airy and uncluttered to maintain the brand’s natural, breathable feel.

8. Tone & Microcopy
Tone Attributes

Friendly but not childish

Helpful, encouraging, and clear

Avoid botanical jargon unless meaningful

Microcopy Examples

“Add a plant to your garden”

“Share progress with your community”

“Tasks to help your garden thrive”

Avoid dry or technical phrasing.
